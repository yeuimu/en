Yunnan Trip Chinese

2024-09-01 4 min 772 words Diary

Day 1: Arrival at Dali!

flowchart LR
    广州南站 -- 高铁 4H ---> 大理站

Arrive Dali already PM 7, but the sky very bright.💡

While walking to bus stop, I noticed a bunch of older ladies doing square dancing, and their music choices were unlike those in Guangzhou.

The inn situated close to east gate of Dali Old Town, just 200 meters from bus stop. We took bus No. 8 for about an hour.

flowchart LR
    下关 -- 8路公交 1H ---> 大理东门

After checking in, We had delicious wild mushroom hotpot at Jun Guo Fu and then back to inn where had a good night’s sleep.💤️

Day 2: Around Erhai!

We rented electric scooter and toured around Erhai.

flowchart TB
    A[大理古城] --> 龙龛码头 --> 廊桥 --> 喜洲古镇 --> 周城 --> A

These attractions are all around Erhai, such as S-shaped bend are similar. It’s enough to visit only one place.✊

Finally, we did tie-dye in Zhoucheng for 88￥, including a skirt and doing tie-dye by ourself.👍

We had terrible lunch at private restaurant in Xizhou Old Town!💢

However, we had delicious Dai Hand-grabbed Rice dinner back in the Dali Old Town.😁
中午在喜洲古镇吃了一顿非常难吃的私房菜💢！不过晚上回去古城吃了一顿傣族手抓饭😁

Day 3: To Lijiang!

flowchart LR
    大理古城苍山公交站 -- 滴滴站点巴士 3H ---> 丽江古城南门

The inn is very close from south gate of Lijiang Old Town, then this time we the accommodation is quite good.

In the evening, walking around Lijiang Old Town and Zhongyi Market.

The south gate of Lijiang Old Town is very quiet, full of wooden house which side fill with flowers and succulents.







The Zhongyi Market is very lively.

Finally, Don’t forget to grab the cable tickets for Jade Dragon Snow Mountain at 8 PM, you won’t be able to go to top of one.

Day 4: Up to Jade Dragon Snow Mountain!

flowchart LR
    忠义市场 -- 101公交 40M ---> 东巴谷 -- 拉客司机 10M ---> 玉龙雪山游客中心

It don’t have to see Sunlit Golden Mountain arriving Dongba valley, if it’s cloudy⛅ or rainy🌧. Because of it’s hard to appear in that case.

This time it didn’t appear due to cloudy.🥺

flowchart LR
    游客中心 --> 蓝月谷 --> 3号停车场 --> 冰川公园索道

Jade Dragon Snow Mountain has 4 attractions: Glacier Park(top of the mountain), Blue Moon Valley, Yunshanping, Youtouping. Apart from Blue Moon Valley, the rest require an admission fee.

This time, only go to Glacier Park and Blue Moon Valley, but took a day…

Day 5: Travel Shooting~📸

We stayed in Lijiang Old Town whole day, on the one hand, had tired and had a little altitude sickness due to climb the mountain yesterday, on the other hand, can take travel shooting, in addition walking rest of old town(the north gate and east gate).


Day 6: To Shangri-La!

flowchart LR
    忠义市场 -- 16路或4路公交 40M ---> 丽江站 -- 高铁 1H ---> 香格里拉站

I made decision here; I should taken the station bus. Additionally, The bus is so slow and crowded !!😡

We got off train station Shangri-La just now, and had Yak Meat Hotpot🍲 with native highland barley cake🫓 and crispy meat tea.🍵

Day 7: Touring at Napahai

In the morning, we got Tibetan makeup and costume, rented electric scooter to tour at Napahai.

First, we navigated Road Arrounding Lake, which with prairie, wetland, lake, layer by layer.

Then, we navigated Giardino Botanico Alpinia of Shangri-La, which can surround whole picture of Napahai.

This time, we got up late, departing from 2 PM, so not able to go to Ganden Sumtseling Monastery, just go tomorrow.

In the evening, we toured at Big Prayer Wheel in Old Town, finally booked the tickets to Potatso National Park.🎟

Day 8: Touring Potatso National Park and Ganden Sumtseling Monastery

It doesn’t make strategies to go to Potatso National Park, because of explanations of touring way repeatedly by tour guaide on inside bus

In the afternoon, made time to Ganden Sumtseling Monastery🛕, which walked over quickly due to it’s so small.

In the night, bought tickets to back in Guangzhou.🎟
